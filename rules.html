LET'S BE HAVING YOU

Game Rules

PAY TEN OF YOUR FINEST ENGLISH POUNDS TO ENTER.

AT THE START OF EACH GAME WEEK PICK A TEAM YOU THINK WILL WIN. YOU CAN ONLY PICK EACH TEAM ONCE THROUGHOUT THE COMPETITION OTHER THAN 2 PRE-SELECTED TEAMS THAT YOU CAN PICK TWICE.

WIN POINTS FOR YOUR SELECTED TEAM'S RESULT IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE TABLE BELOW. THE TEAMS ARE SPLIT INTO QUARTILES BASED ON LEAGUE POSITION AT THE START OF THE GAME WEEK. POINTS ARE AWARDED BASED ON HOW DARING YOUR PICK IS. AWAY WINS AGAINST HIGHER QUARTILE TEAMS ARE MORE VALUABLE THAN HOME WINS AGAINST LOWER QUARTILE TEAMS. BE BRAVE - COWARDICE WILL GET YOU NOWHERE!

EACH WEEK YOU ALSO PICK A PLAYER AND THEY ARE AWARDED POINTS FOR ANY GOALS AND ASSISTS THEY GET. USE HAALAND WISELY!

THE COMPETITION WILL LAST UNTIL THE END OF THE SEASON. THE PERSON WITH THE MOST POINTS WINS. WHAT CONSTITUTES A GAMEWEEK IS AT THE ORGANISER'S DISCRETION AND MAY NOT FOLLOW OTHER POPULAR FANTASY GAMES.
